What We Do

Core Capabilities

Seven disciplines. One mandate: make the business perform.

01

Commercial Transformation

End-to-end commercial redesign — market positioning, revenue model, pricing architecture, and go-to-market execution. Not strategy documents. Live operating changes.

02

GTM Architecture & Revenue Design

Building and rebuilding the revenue engine — channel strategy, pricing, sales architecture, pipeline systems, and end-to-end RevOps. From blueprint to execution.

03

Fractional CGO / CRO Leadership

Senior operating authority in the C-suite without the full-time commitment. We own revenue, lead teams, and make decisions — inside your business, accountable for results.

04

PE Portfolio Operations

Embedded commercial operators for PE-backed companies. Value creation plan execution, EBITDA acceleration, exit readiness, and board advisory across the hold period.

05

Marketing Due Diligence

Operator-grade commercial diligence pre and post-close. Market sizing, competitive collision analysis, GTM risk mapping, and channel quality assessment.

06

Turnaround & Performance Repair

Fast diagnosis and decisive intervention when growth stalls, leadership turns over, or execution breaks down. Results in 90 days. Not 90-page reports.

07

Scale with Operational AI

Deploying AI as a commercial operating lever — not a science project. AI-powered diligence, GTM analytics, MarTech optimization, and RevOps automation at scale.
