About

MonarchX Capital is a selective operating practice.

We step into companies at critical inflection points — turnarounds, stalled growth, broken GTM, or leadership transitions — and operate alongside the team to drive clarity, velocity, and results.

We don't consult. We operate.

MonarchX serves as a force-multiplier for founders, PE sponsors, and executive teams navigating complexity at the $100M–$3B revenue range. We bring senior operating capability — not slides, not theory — directly into the business.

We work best with leaders who know they need operators, not advisors.

MonarchX was founded by Christopher Loughlin, an operator with 20+ years leading commercial strategy and turnarounds across enterprise, PE-backed, and growth-stage companies in payments, fintech, healthcare, and SaaS. The firm works directly with enterprise leaders, PE sponsors, boards, CEOs, CROs, and CMOs as embedded operators and board advisors.
