# MonarchX Capital — Keyword & Market Research
*February 2026*

## Primary Keyword Clusters

### 1. Fractional Executive (HIGH VOLUME, HIGH COMPETITION)
- fractional CGO / fractional chief growth officer
- fractional CRO / fractional chief revenue officer
- fractional CMO (highest volume in this cluster)
- fractional C-suite for PE portfolio companies
- fractional executive services
- **Trend:** Booming. Blended roles (CMO+CRO+CGO) increasingly common. PE-backed and VC-backed companies driving adoption. MonarchX should own "fractional CGO" and "embedded operator" — less contested than "fractional CMO."

### 2. PE Portfolio Operations (HIGH INTENT, LOW COMPETITION)
- PE operating partner services
- PE portfolio company turnaround
- PE value creation consulting
- EBITDA acceleration consulting
- portfolio company commercial transformation
- exit readiness consulting
- commercial due diligence PE
- **Trend:** PE firms increasingly building or outsourcing operating teams. PwC reports this as a major structural shift. Terms like "value creation plan," "portco transformation," "operating partner" are the language PE uses.

### 3. Turnaround & Transformation (MEDIUM VOLUME, MEDIUM COMPETITION)
- turnaround consulting / turnaround operations
- commercial transformation consulting
- business turnaround specialist
- performance improvement consulting
- revenue turnaround
- **Trend:** AlixPartners, Alvarez & Marsal, FTI dominate the large-cap space. Mid-market ($100M-$3B) is underserved — MonarchX's sweet spot.

### 4. GTM Strategy (HIGH VOLUME, HIGH COMPETITION)
- GTM strategy consulting
- go-to-market strategy
- GTM rebuild
- revenue architecture
- sales architecture consulting
- GTM due diligence
- **Trend:** "GTM rebuild" and "GTM architecture" are differentiated terms vs. generic "GTM strategy." MonarchX already uses these well.

### 5. AI-Enabled Operations (EMERGING, LOW COMPETITION)
- AI transformation consulting
- operational AI consulting
- AI-powered due diligence
- AI GTM optimization
- MarTech transformation
- AEO consulting / answer engine optimization
- AI commercial strategy
- **Trend:** Exploding in 2025-2026. "Operational AI" (as opposed to "AI consulting") is a differentiated positioning. AEO/GEO are brand-new categories — first-mover advantage available.

### 6. Special Situations / Niche
- special situations consulting
- leadership transition consulting
- commercial reset
- stalled growth turnaround
- broken GTM fix

## Buyer Persona Search Behavior

### PE Operating Partners / Principals
Search: "portfolio company turnaround consultant," "commercial due diligence firm," "embedded operator PE," "operating partner resources"
Intent: Finding operators to deploy into portcos. Value speed, track record, confidentiality.

### CEOs / Founders ($100M-$3B)
Search: "fractional CGO," "GTM not working," "revenue growth stalled," "need commercial leadership"
Intent: Solving an acute problem. Want someone who's done it before, not a framework.

### Board Members
Search: "turnaround operator," "interim commercial leadership," "special situations advisor"
Intent: Crisis or transition. Need credibility signals (track record, references).
